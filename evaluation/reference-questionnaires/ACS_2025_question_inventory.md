# American Community Survey (ACS) 2025 - Question Inventory

## Document Overview
- **Title**: American Community Survey
- **Organization**: United States Census Bureau
- **Form Number**: ACS-1(INFO)(2025)
- **Date**: 02-28-2024 (form date)
- **OMB Numbers**: 0607-0810, 0607-0936
- **Pages**: 48 (informational copy shows pp. 1-19, 48; pp. 20-47 repeat Person 1 questions for Persons 2-5)
- **Language**: English
- **Type**: Household survey covering demographics, housing, and socioeconomic characteristics. Mandatory by law.

## Structure

The ACS 2025 questionnaire is organized into the following major sections:

1. **Cover Page** (p. 1): Respondent contact info, household count
2. **Person Roster - Detailed (Persons 1-5)** (pp. 2-7): Name, relationship, sex, age/DOB, Hispanic origin, race (6 questions each for Persons 1-5; Persons 6-12 abbreviated on p. 7)
3. **Person Roster - Abbreviated (Persons 6-12)** (p. 7): Name, sex, age only
4. **Housing** (pp. 8-11): Building type, year built, move-in date, acreage, agriculture, rooms, plumbing, utilities, internet, vehicles, energy, costs, tenure, mortgage/rent
5. **Person 1 Detailed Questions** (pp. 12-18): Place of birth, citizenship, year of entry, education, ancestry, language, residence 1 year ago, health insurance, disability, fertility, grandparents, marital status, military service, employment, commuting, income
6. **Persons 2-5 Detailed Questions** (pp. 19-47): Same as Person 1 questions (repeated identically for each person)
7. **Mailing Instructions** (p. 48)
