# European Social Survey Round 12 (ESS12) - Question Inventory

## Document Overview
- **Title**: ESS12 Source Questionnaire
- **Organization**: European Social Survey (ESS) ERIC, City, University of London
- **Date**: Round 12 (fieldwork period not specified in document; document version for ESS Round 12)
- **Pages**: 104 pages (including cover pages, instructions, and footnotes)
- **Language**: English (source questionnaire)
- **Type**: Cross-national social survey questionnaire; multi-mode (Face-to-face / Self-completion Web / Self-completion Paper)

## Structure

The ESS12 source questionnaire is organized into the following sections:

- **Section S**: Screening/selection questions (SC Web and Paper only) -- 6 questions
- **Section A**: Core module -- Media, social trust, politics, government, immigration, well-being, religion, demographics, identity -- 89+ questions
- **Section B**: Core module -- Household composition, demographics, education, employment, income, parental background -- 60+ questions
- **Section C**: Rotating module -- Personal and social well-being (CES-D depression scale, eudaimonic well-being, social harmony, compassion) -- 30 questions
- **Section D**: Rotating module -- Immigration attitudes (detailed) -- 30+ questions
- **Section E**: Core module -- Human Values Scale (Schwartz PVQ-RR, 20 items) -- 20 questions
- **Section F**: Survey experience questions (SC Web and Paper only) -- 6 questions
- **Section G**: Recontact and incentive collection -- 6+ questions
- **Section H**: Incentive delivery details (SC Web and Paper only) -- 3 questions
- **Section J**: Interviewer questionnaire (F2F only) -- 10 questions
