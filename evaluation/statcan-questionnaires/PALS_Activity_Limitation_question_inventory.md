# Participation and Activity Limitation Survey (PALS) 2001 - Question Inventory
# Form 03: Children (Under 15)

## Document Overview
- **Title**: Participation and Activity Limitation Survey -- 2001 (Children -- under 15)
- **Organization**: Statistics Canada
- **Date**: 2001
- **Pages**: 53
- **Language**: English
- **Type**: Interviewer-administered household survey questionnaire (Form 03)
- **Catalogue**: STC/HFS-110-75174 (8-4500-113.1)
- **Authority**: Collected under the authority of the Statistics Act, Statutes of Canada, 1985, Chapter S19

## Structure
The questionnaire consists of a cover page with respondent demographics and information source, followed by 9 substantive sections (A through I), a follow-up section, and administrative records:

- **Section A** -- Filter Questions (general activity limitation screening)
- **Section B** -- Activity Limitations (hearing, seeing, communicating, walking, hands/fingers, learning, developmental, emotional/psychological, chronic conditions, health, medications, costs, health services, aids/equipment)
- **Section C** -- Help with Everyday Activities (personal care, mobility, family help, coordination of care, work impacts, finances)
- **Section D** -- Child Care (arrangements, refusals)
- **Section E** -- Education (school type, grade, special education, school activities, building features, assistive aids, parental involvement, transportation, professional assessment) -- NOT asked if child born after May 15, 1996
- **Section F** -- Leisure and Recreation Activities (sports, media, reading, social, camps, barriers, computers, Internet) -- NOT asked if child born after May 15, 1996
- **Section G** -- Home Accommodation (exterior/interior special features) -- NOT asked if child born after May 15, 1996
- **Section H** -- Transportation (car features, specialized bus, taxi, costs) -- NOT asked if child born after May 15, 1996
- **Section I** -- Economic Characteristics (insurance, tax credits, income)
- **Follow-up** -- Contact information for future surveys
